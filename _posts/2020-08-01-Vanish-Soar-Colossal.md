## 1. Vanish, Disappear

Cả 2 từ trên đều mang nghĩa là biến mất.

**Vanish**: sudden, suprising disapear (less formal)

Ex: *She vanished into the thin air*

**Disappear**

Ex: *The sun disappeared slowly below the horizon.*

## 2. Soar = Rise quickly

Ex: *Temperatures will soar over the weekend, say the weather forecasters.*

## 3. Colossal = Extremely large

Ex: *They were asking a colossal amount of money for the house.*

## 4. Add up = Calculate the total of prices

Ex: *He quickly added up the figures in his head.*

## 5. Come up with = Think of

Ex: *He's come up with a great idea.*

## 6. Carry out = Accomplish

Chúng kều mang nghĩa là tiến hành.

Ex: *He carry out the plan.*

## 7. Weather and Climate

**Climate** is the **Weather** conditions of a region.

## 8. Break away = Escape from control

Ex: *The dog broke away from its owner.*
